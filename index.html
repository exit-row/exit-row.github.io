The NBA's Boston Celtics posted a picture of their player, Jaylen Brown, on their Instagram wearing a design I made that's available for purchase on different items via RedBubble.com

You can see the post here, with the third slide being the picture in reference.

I had an idea for a joke t-shirt, but I figured it'd be funnier if instead of just Photoshopping it all together and having a pixelated raster image, I would make it from scratch in Illustrator. That way not only would the quality be top-notch but the art style would be consistent.
