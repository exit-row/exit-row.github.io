Pen & Pixel was an iconic design firm that designed over 19,000 album covers over a span of 11 years. Although their designs were generally described as gaudy, featuring lots of bling, wild colors and outrageous scenarios, they were one of the defining elements of 90s hip hop - at one point it was rare for a southern artist not to have a cover made by them.

This single cover was a callback to those designs, but a little more refined.
